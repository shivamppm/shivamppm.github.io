Hostel Rules & Regulations
All residents must read, understand, and comply with these guidelines.
Gate opens 5:30 AM
Breakfast 7:00–8:30 AM

Lights off 11:00 PM
Attendance 8:30 PM

General Discipline
  • Maintain dignity and good conduct at all times
  • Respect staff, wardens, faculty and fellow residents
  • Ragging is strictly prohibited by government regulation
  • Violence, bullying or harassment leads to strict action
Attendance & Entry
  • Return to hostel by 9:00 PM daily
  • Late entry requires prior Warden permission
  • Daily attendance taken at designated time
  • Sign movement register when leaving / returning
Visitors Policy
  • Visitors allowed only during specified hours
  • No visitors permitted inside hostel rooms
  • Parents must register at hostel office first
  • Female visitors not allowed on premises
Room Maintenance
  • Keep your room clean at all times
  • Use hostel property with care
  • Damage costs charged to the student
  • No room change without written permission
Study Environment
  • Silence during designated study hours
  • No loud music, shouting or disturbances
  • Cooperate in maintaining academic atmosphere
Prohibited Activities
  • No alcohol, tobacco, drugs or intoxicants
  • No gambling, betting or unauthorized parties
  • No weapons, explosives or hazardous materials
  • No cooking inside hostel rooms
Electrical & Safety
  • No heaters, induction cookers or immersion rods
  • Switch off all appliances when leaving room
  • No tampering with electrical fittings
  • Follow fire safety instructions always
Mess Rules
  • Follow mess timings strictly
  • Avoid food wastage at all times
  • Handle utensils and property carefully
  • Maintain cleanliness in the dining hall
Mobile & Internet
  • Phones must not disturb other residents
  • No illegal or inappropriate online content
  • Wi-Fi for academic and lawful use only
Violations may result in:
Warning
Fine
Suspension
Expulsion
Institutional Action
All students and parents must sign an undertaking agreeing to comply with all rules.
