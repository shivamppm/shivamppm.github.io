Why This Course?
In the next 5 years, every job — from business to engineering to media — will demand AI literacy. This course gives you a practical, hands-on skillset in Generative AI: the technology behind ChatGPT, DALL-E, GitHub Copilot, and Gemini. You will learn to use AI tools, build chatbots, create content, and prompt AI like an expert — skills that make your resume stand out before you even graduate. No prior coding required.
