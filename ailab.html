ANA AI Lab  —  Rules & Guidelines for Students
✅
Do's
  • 1
    Follow all instructions given by the faculty and lab instructor before using AI systems.
  • 2
    Use AI tools responsibly for academic learning, research, and approved educational activities only.
  • 3
    Maintain discipline, keep the workstation clean, and handle all equipment with care.
  • 4
    Save your work regularly and log out of all accounts before leaving the lab.
  • 5
    Report any hardware, software, or network issues immediately to the lab instructor.
❌
Don'ts
  • 1
    Do not install, uninstall, or modify any software or system settings without prior permission.
  • 2
    Do not access inappropriate, illegal, or unauthorized websites, applications, or AI services.
  • 3
    Do not misuse AI tools for plagiarism, cheating, or generating unethical or harmful content.
  • 4
    Do not connect unauthorized USB drives, external devices, or personal software to lab computers.
  • 5
    Do not eat, drink, create unnecessary noise, or disturb other students while inside the AI Lab.
